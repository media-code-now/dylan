Your security comes first!

There’s no place in the world that should feel safer than your own home. With Chief of Locks, you can be sure that you are getting the most safe and secure lock system you can! Our experienced technicians can help you choose the right lock and security system for your home.

Here’s what we do to keep you safe

If you’re just moving to a new house or even have been living in the same one for a while, changing the locks on your home is always a great idea. To prevent any unwanted guests and intruders, Chief of Locks provides these residential services:

  • Rekey
  • Lock Change
  • Deadlock Installation
  • Keypad Lock Installation and Programming
  • Key and Lock Disposal
  • Security System Consultation
